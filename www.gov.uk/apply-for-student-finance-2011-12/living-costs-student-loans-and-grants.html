Part 3: Living costs - student loans and grants

Student loans and grants are available to help with living costs.

Full-time students

Full-time students can get the following:

  • Maintenance Loan
  • Maintenance Grant

Maintenance Loan

The Maintenance Loan has to be repaid. How much you get depends on:

  • your household income
  • where you live
  • how much Maintenance Grant you get
  • whether you’re in your final year (when you get slightly less)

You can get 72% of the loan without having your household income assessed - to get the full loan your household income needs to be assessed.

You can get the following:

Where you live when studying The maximum loan
At home £3,838
Away from home outside London £4,950
Away from home in London £6,928

The money is paid to the university or college at the beginning of term once you’ve registered on your course.

Maintenance Grant

The Maintenance Grant doesn’t have to be repaid. How much you get depends on your household income so you need to have this assessed when you apply.

You can get:

Household income Maintenance Grant
Under £25,000 £2,906
£25,001-£50,020 Partial grant
Over £50,020 No grant

If you get a Maintenance Grant you get a smaller Maintenance Loan, meaning you have less to repay.

The money is paid into your bank account each term once you’ve registered on your course.

Part-time students

Part-time students can apply for a Course Grant.

How much you get depends on:

  • your household income
  • whether you have children
  • whether you have a partner

The Course Grant is paid into your bank account each term once you’ve registered on your course.

If you’re single without children you can get up to:

Household income Course Grant
Up to £26,029 £265
£26,030-£28,065 £50-£265
Over £28,065 No grant

If you have a partner or children you can still get the grant if you earn an extra:

  • £2,000 if you have a partner whose income is taken into account
  • £2,000 for the first of your or your partner’s dependent children
  • £1,000 for any other dependent children
