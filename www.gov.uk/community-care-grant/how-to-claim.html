Part 4: How to claim

Send form SF300 to your local Jobcentre Plus. The form includes notes to help you fill it out.

Download ‘Form SF300 - Community Care Grant and Crisis Loan applications’ (PDF, 264KB)

If you’re unhappy with a decision

You can ask for the decision about your grant to be reviewed if you’re unhappy with it. To do this, contact Jobcentre Plus within 28 days of their decision letter.

If you’re still unhappy after the review you can ask the independent Social Fund Inspector to review the decision.

Download ‘How to ask for an independent review’ (PDF, 80KB)
