Part 3: Eligibility

Employees qualify if they:

  • have been in the company for more than a year
  • are named on the child’s birth or adoption certificate
  • have parental responsibility
  • are not self-employed or a ‘worker’ - eg an agency worker or contractor
  • are not a foster parent

Employers can ask for proof (like a birth certificate) as long as it reasonable to do so - eg they can’t ask for proof each time an employee requests leave.

Employers can extend parental leave to those groups who aren’t eligible. Employees can check this in their staff handbook.
