National standard for driving buses and coaches (category D)

The standard sets out what the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) believes is needed to be a safe and responsible bus or coach driver.

Only applies to England, Scotland and Wales

Introduction

The standard describes the skills, knowledge and understanding needed to be a safe and responsible driver of a category D vehicle.

Changes to the standard

DSA updates the standard on a regular basis. It’s updated straight away if any safety critical issues are identified. You can keep up to date with new versions by email.
