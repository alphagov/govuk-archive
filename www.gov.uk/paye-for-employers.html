Quick answer Running your business' payroll

Get guidance on managing your payroll, from setting up as new employer to what you need to do at the end of each tax year.

You must use the new Real Time Information system if HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) have told you to.

If HMRC haven’t contacted you, you must keep using the standard PAYE system.

Action Standard PAYE system Real Time Information
Set up your payroll Getting started with PAYE Getting started with PAYE in real time
Day-to-day payroll management Payroll, calculations and form filling Payroll
