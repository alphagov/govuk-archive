Public Request to Order Disposal: request form

This form helps people to exercise their rights under the Community Right to Reclaim Land to bring disused property back into beneficial use

Only applies to England

This form is provided for applicants to help exercise your right under the Community Right to Reclaim Land to seek to bring under-used or unused land or property back into a beneficial use.
