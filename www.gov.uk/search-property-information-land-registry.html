Service Search for property information from the Land Registry

Find information about a property, even if you don’t own it. Search by address to find the owner, how far its general boundaries extend and whether it’s likely to be a flood risk.

What you need to know

Start now on the Land Registry website

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know

Title plan

A title plan shows the property’s general boundaries. You can download a copy for £4.

Title register

This shows who currently owns the property, how much they bought it for (if bought after April 2000) and any rights of way or restrictions on the way the land can be used. You can download a copy for £4.

Flood Risk Indicator

This report costs £9 plus VAT and estimates how likely the property is to flood in the future.

  • Available in England and Wales only
  • Debit or credit card required
