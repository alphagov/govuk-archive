Quick answer Driving test costs

A car theory test costs £31 and the car practical test normally costs £62 - the full rates for different vehicles are shown in the tables.

These are the prices to book your test using the official service. Unofficial websites may charge more.

Car

Test type Weekday Evening, weekend and bank holiday
Theory test £31 £31
Practical test £62 £75

Motorcycle

Test type Weekday Evening, weekend and bank holiday
Theory test £31 £31
Practical module 1 (off-road) test £15.50 £15.50
Practical module 2 (on-road) test £75 £88.50

Lorry, bus and coach

Test type Weekday Evening, weekend and bank holiday
Theory: multiple-choice test £35 £35
Theory: hazard perception test £15 £15
Theory: Driver Certificate of Professional Competence (CPC) case studies test £30 £30
Practical: vocational test £115 £141
Practical: Driver CPC practical test (fee includes driver qualification card) £55 £63

Car and trailer

Test type Weekday Evening, weekend and bank holiday
Practical test £115 £141

Tractor and other specialist vehicles

Test type Weekday Evening, weekend and bank holiday
Practical test £62 £75

Taxi and private hire

Test type Weekday Evening, weekend and bank holiday
Hackney saloon vehicles and private hire saloon vehicles £79.66 £96
Hackney wheelchair accessible vehicles £92.94 £112.34
Taxi wheelchair exercise £26.56 £32.68

You’ll have to pay the full amount again if you don’t pass your test and want to take another one.
