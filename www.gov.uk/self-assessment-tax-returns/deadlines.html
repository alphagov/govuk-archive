Part 3: Deadlines

The 2011 to 2012 tax year ended 5 April 2012. The main deadlines are:

  • paper tax returns - midnight 31 October 2012
  • online tax returns - midnight 31 January 2013
  • final payment of any tax due - midnight 31 January 2013

If you’ve missed the paper deadline, send your return online instead to avoid a penalty.

Exceptions

The deadline for sending the tax return can be different.

Example
30 December - If you owe less than £3,000, send your return online and want HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) to try and collect the tax through your tax code.

Example
31 January - paper tax returns for trustees of registered pension schemes or non-resident companies liable to Income Tax (because you can’t do these online).

Example
HMRC writes telling you a different date.

There are additional payment deadlines if you make ‘payments on account’.

You can pay HM Revenue & Customs in different ways, including direct debit and internet banking.
