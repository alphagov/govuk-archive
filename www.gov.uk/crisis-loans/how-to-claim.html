Part 4: How to claim

Living expenses and urgent rent in advance

To apply for living expenses and urgent rent in advance contact Jobcentre Plus.

Decisions are usually made on the day you apply. Payment is usually made within a few hours, depending on whether you collect it from the Jobcentre or if it’s paid into your bank account.

Jobcentre Plus
Telephone: 0800 032 7952
Textphone: 0800 032 7958
Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm

Other types of application

For any other type of application send or take a claim form to your local Jobcentre Plus.

Download ‘Crisis Loans, items and services in the event of a disaster - Form SF300’ (PDF, 264KB)

Download ‘Crisis Loans, day-to-day living expenses - Form SF401’ (PDF, 157KB)

Download ‘Crisis Loans, non-urgent rent in advance - Form SF401R’ (PDF, 169KB)

You can also get these forms from your local Jobcentre Plus.

Reviewing a decision on your application

If you’re unhappy with the decision on your Crisis Loan application you’ve got the right to ask for a review. You must write to Jobcentre Plus within 28 days of the decision, explaining why you think it was wrong.

If, after the review, you still think the decision was wrong you can ask for a further review by a Social Fund Inspector. This review should take no longer than 21 working days. You can download a leaflet and claim form to ask for an independent review.

Download ‘How to ask for an independent review’ (PDF, 2.4MB)
