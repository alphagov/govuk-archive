Military equipment, logistics and technology

Procurement of equipment, military technology and readiness for operations.

  1. Acquisition Operating Framework

    The Acquisition Operating Framework is the main source of policy and guidance on acquisition for the Ministry of Defence and industry partners.

  2. Business continuity

    Business continuity is the agreed process to ensure the continuation of critical functions in the event of a major disruption.

  3. Buying defence equipment from the UK armed forces

    Find out about defence equipment that is no longer needed or being sold on by the UK armed forces.

  4. Commercial sale of Type 22 frigates

    Four former British warships are up for sale. The deadline for interested parties is 23 January 2013 with viewing inspections in February and March.

  5. Crichel Down Rules on land ownership

    Rules that apply to the sale of sites previously acquired by government departments and in defence.

  6. Crown immunity of controlled military and dual-use items

    Exemptions and export waivers for goods owned by the Crown that are otherwise controlled military or dual-use goods.

  7. Defence Imagery

    Contains stills, video, audio, and press releases relating to current Ministry of Defence stories.

  8. Defence Intelligence

    Defence Intelligence provides strategic defence intelligence to the Ministry of Defence and the Armed Forces.

  9. Defence Safety and Environment Authority

    This is the agency that is responsible for the regulation of defence safety.

  10. Defence technology centres

    Working on behalf of the Ministry of Defence, the 4 Defence Technology Centres bring together academics and specialists from industry.

  11. Depleted uranium

    The Ministry of Defence's policy on using depleted uranium in weapons and how we monitor munitions and health and environmental risk.

  12. Development concepts and doctrine centre

    The development, concepts and doctrine centre (DCDC) is a Ministry of Defence think-tank, linked to the Defence Academy at Shrivenham.

  13. Exporting military goods to the United States

    Find out more about the International Traffic in Arms Regulations and the US-UK Defence Trade Co-operation Treaty.

  14. MOD Architecture Framework

    The MOD Architecture Framework (MODAF) is a set of rules that support defence planning and change management activities.

  15. Military Stabilisation Support Group

    The MSSG's regular and reserve Armed Forces personnel help civilians stabilise countries emerging from conflict or at risk.

  16. Ministry of Defence (MOD) F680 applications

    Uses of the Ministry of Defence's classified information Form 680, when you need one, how to get one and where to send it.

  17. Ministry of Defence across the UK

    The MOD has dozens of major defence sites spread across the UK and provides jobs and work to many thousands of people.

  18. Ministry of Defence: byelaws

    The Byelaws Review Team has been set up specifically to carry out an over-reaching review of the Ministry of Defence’s byelaw requirements.

  19. National security vetting for the Armed Services

    DBS NSV provides national security vetting to the 3 Armed Services and other customers across the public sector.

  20. Operations in Iraq

    UK military support operations in Iraq continue to be conducted under the name 'Operation TELIC'.

  21. Service Prosecuting Authority

    Reviews cases referred by the Service Police and Chain of Command and prosecutes cases at Court Martial or Service Civilian Court.

  22. Sharing defence spectrum

    The Ministry of Defence intends to open up some military frequency bands for new sharing opportunities for public and private sector users.

  23. The Defence Scientific Advisory Council

    The DSAC gives independent advice to the Secretary of State for Defence on science, engineering, technology and analysis.
