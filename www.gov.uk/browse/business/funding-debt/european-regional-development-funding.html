European regional development funding

Advice on the potential of projects and their eligibility for funding from European Regional Development Fund (ERDF), including information on applying for funding in specific region.

  1. Applying for ERDF funding

    How to apply for ERDF funding.

  2. ERDF regional guidance: Cornwall and the Isles of Scilly

    Cornwall and the Isles of Scilly has been allocated €458.1 million of ERDF funding to invest in projects that will support regional businesses.

  3. ERDF regional guidance: East Midlands

    The East Midlands is one of 9 English regions to qualify for Competitiveness and Employment funding.

  4. ERDF regional guidance: East of England

    The East of England has been allocated €110.9 million to invest in projects that will support regional businesses and create jobs.

  5. ERDF regional guidance: London

    London is one of 9 regions to qualify for Competitiveness and Employment funding.

  6. ERDF regional guidance: North East

    The North East is one of 9 regions to qualify for Competitiveness and Employment funding.

  7. ERDF regional guidance: North West

    The North West is one of 9 regions to qualify for Competitiveness and Employment funding.

  8. ERDF regional guidance: South East

    The South East been allocated €47.4 million of European Regional Development Funding to invest in projects that will support regional businesses and create jobs.

  9. ERDF regional guidance: South West

    The South West been allocated €124.7 million of ERDF to invest in projects that will support regional businesses and create jobs.

  10. ERDF regional guidance: West Midlands

    The West Midlands has been allocated €400 million of European Regional Development Funding to invest in projects that will support regional businesses and create jobs.

  11. ERDF: national guidance

    The Department for Communities and Local Government (DCLG) has adopted a standardised approach to the management and delivery of the ERDF Programmes.
