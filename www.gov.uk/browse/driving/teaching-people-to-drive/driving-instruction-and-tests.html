Driving instruction and tests

National standards for driving all vehicles types, responsibilities of approved driving instructors, motorbike training syllabus and getting approval to host driving tests.

  1. Compulsory basic training (CBT) syllabus and guidance notes

    The CBT syllabus and guidance notes set out what is required of motorcycle trainers and learner riders.

  2. Direct access scheme (DAS) motorcycle training guidance

    The direct access scheme (DAS) guidance notes set out what should happen during DAS training.

  3. Drink drive rehabilitation scheme: guide for course providers and courts

    This guide sets out the minimum requirements for drink drive rehabilitation courses for course providers.

  4. Drink-drive rehabilitation syllabus

    The drink-drive rehabilitation syllabus sets out what should be covered on drink-drive rehabilitation courses.

  5. Driving tests: rules for using your own site

    You can host driving tests at your own site if it meets certain rules and is approved by the Driving Standards Agency (DSA).

  6. Guide to the approved driving instructor register

    The guide to the approved driving instructor (ADI) register (known as the 'ADI14') sets out what's involved in becoming an ADI.

  7. Licensed use of DSA's theory test learning materials

    The Driving Standards Agency (DSA) encourages the reuse of its official learning materials with a licence agreement.

  8. Motorcycle approved training body (ATB) manual

    This manual gives detailed information about setting up and running an approved training body (ATB) to provide motorcycle training.

  9. National standard for driver and rider training

    The standard sets out what the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) believes is needed to provide a programme of driver and rider training.

  10. National standard for driving buses and coaches (category D)

    The standard sets out what the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) believes is needed to be a safe and responsible bus or coach driver.

  11. National standard for driving cars (category B)

    The standard sets out what the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) believes is needed to be a safe and responsible car driver.

  12. National standard for driving lorries (category C)

    The standard sets out what the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) believes is needed to be a safe and responsible lorry driver.

  13. National standard for riding mopeds and motorcycles (category A or P)

    The standard sets out what the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) believes is needed to be a safe and responsible moped or motorcycle rider.

  14. Rules for using DSA logos

    This guide explains how to apply to the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) for permission to use its logos and the rules you must follow.
