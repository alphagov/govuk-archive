Jobseeker's Allowance and low income benefits

  • Jobseeker's Allowance

    Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA) - including contribution-based JSA, income-based JSA, who is eligible, what you get and how to claim (including rapid reclaim)

  • Contact Jobcentre Plus

    Find your local Jobcentre Plus, make a new or repeat benefit claim over the phone, search for a job over the phone

  • Income Support

    Income Support is extra money to help people on a low income or none at all - rates, eligibility and how to claim

  • Community Care Grants

    Community Care Grants - money to help you set up home after leaving care or when your family is in exceptional need - what you'll get, apply, eligibility

  • Budgeting Loans

    Budgeting loans can help to pay for essential everyday costs - what you'll get, eligibility and how to apply

  • Crisis Loans

    Crisis Loans can help with costs after an emergency or disaster - what you'll get, eligibility, apply

  • Carer's Allowance

    Carer's Allowance can help if you look after someone who needs to be cared for - what you'll get, eligibility, apply, claim for DS700

  • Disability Premiums (Income Support)

    A disability premium is extra money if you’re under 60 with health problems that stop you from working - what you get, eligibility, apply

  • Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)

    Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) money if you can't work because of illness or disability - rates, eligibility, apply, assessment

  • Legal aid eligibility calculator

    Use this tool to check if you can get legal aid, which can help pay for legal advice, mediation or representation in court if you can’t afford it

  • Carer's Allowance Unit

    Contact information for Carer's Allowance Unit, telephone number and address

  • Carer's Credit

    Carer's Credit helps carers build up National Insurance contributions for State Pensions - what you'll get, eligibility and how to claim

  • Carers UK

    Get advice and support for carers and the people they care for - telephone, email, opening hours, website

  • Christmas Bonus

    The Christmas Bonus is a one-off tax-free £10 payment to people who get certain benefits - what you'll get, eligibility, how to claim

  • Constant Attendance Allowance

    Constant Attendance Allowance - extra money to help pay for care if you get Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit War or a War Disablement Pension

  • Diffuse Mesothelioma Payment

    You may be able to get a one-off lump sum payment if you're suffering from diffuse mesothelioma, and your exposure to asbestos happened in the UK

  • Help with moving from benefits to work

    Support and services from Jobcentre Plus to help you leave benefits and start work - work programme, work clubs, starting a business, volunteering, work experience, help with drug or alcohol problems, support for carers

  • In Work Credit

    In Work Credit for lone parents leaving benefits and starting work or self-employment - up to £60 a week, claim form, eligibility

  • Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit

    You might get Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit (IIDB) if you're ill or disabled from an accident or disease caused by work - what you'll get, eligibility, how to claim

  • Job Grant

    Job Grant is a one-off payment when you stop benefits and start work - how much you get, eligibility, apply

  • Legal aid

    Legal aid helps pay for legal advice, mediation or representation in court - see if you can claim

  • Mortgage Interest Run On

    Mortgage Interest Run On is extra money you can get towards housing costs for 4 weeks if your benefits are stopping - eligibility, what you'll get, how to claim

  • Reduced Earnings Allowance

    Reduced Earnings Allowance can help if your income falls because of a work-related accident or disease - what you'll get, eligibility and how to claim

  • Return to Work Credit

    A weekly tax-free payment for up to a year If you go back to work after being unable to work for at least 13 weeks because of illness or disability

  • Support for Mortgage Interest (SMI)

    Support for Mortgage Interest (SMI) helps homeowners on certain benefits pay interest on loans or mortgages - what you'll get, eligibility and how to claim
