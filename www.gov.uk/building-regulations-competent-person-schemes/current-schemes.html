Types of building work

Air pressure testing of buildings

The authorised schemes are the British Institute of Non-Destructive Testing (BINDT) and Air Tightness testing and Measurement Association (ATTMA).

Cavity wall insulation in an existing building

Type of insulation Scheme(s)
Cavity walls in an existing building CIGA, Benchmark, NAPIT, NICEIC, Stroma
Internal walls of a building Benchmark, CERTASS, NAPIT, NICEIC, Stroma
External walls of a building (not including insulation of demountable-clad buildings) Benchmark, CERTASS, NAPIT, NICEIC, Stroma
Both external and internal walls of a building (‘hybrid insulation’ - not including insulation of demountable-clad buildings) Benchmark, NAPIT, NICEIC

Combustion appliances

Type of appliance Scheme(s)
Gas Gas Safe Register
Oil (including liquid biofuels) APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, ECA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Solid fuel (including biomass) APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, ECA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC

Electrical installations

Type of installation Schemes
In dwellings - installation of fixed low or extra-low voltage electrical installations Benchmark, BSI, BESCA, ECA, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
In dwellings - fixed low or extra-low voltage electrical installations as part of other work being carried out by the registered person APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, ECA, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Buildings other than dwellings - installation of lighting or electrical heating systems Benchmark, BESCA, ECA, NAPIT, NICEIC, Stroma

Heating and hot water systems

Type of system Schemes
Connected to a heat-producing gas appliance APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, Capita, ECA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Connected to an oil-fired combustion appliance APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, ECA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Connected to a solid fuel appliance APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, ECA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Connected to an electric heat source APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, ECA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Electric heating systems - dwellings Benchmark, BESCA, BSI, ELECSA, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Electric heating systems - buildings other than dwellings BESCA, ELECSA, NAPIT, NICEIC, Stroma

Mechanical ventilation and air-conditioning systems

Type of building Schemes
Dwellings (not including work on systems shared with other buildings Benchmark, BESCA, NAPIT, NICEIC, Stroma
Other buildings (not involving work on other parts of a building occupied separately) Benchmark, BESCA, NAPIT, NICEIC, Stroma

Plumbing and water supply systems

Type of system Schemes
Wholesome and softened wholesome water supply APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC
Non-wholesome water supply to a flushing toilet (not involving work on shared or underground drainage) APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, Stroma
Toilets, sinks, washbasins, fixed baths, showers or bathrooms in dwellings (not involving work on shared or underground drainage) APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, ECA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, Stroma

Replacement windows, doors, roof windows or rooflights

Type of building Schemes
Existing dwellings Fensa, BM Trada, Benchmark, BSI, CERTASS, NAPIT, Network VEKA, Stroma
Existing buildings other than dwellings (not including replacement of load-bearing structural glass, glazed curtain walling or revolving doors) BM Trada, CERTASS, Stroma, Fensa

Replacement of roof coverings on pitched and flat roofs

This includes any necessary connected work (but not solar panel installation). The authorised schemes are:

  • NAPIT
  • NFRC
