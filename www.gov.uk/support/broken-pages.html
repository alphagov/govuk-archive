I can't get to the page I'm trying to visit, can you help?

The most common reason that pages appear to be broken is that the URL has been mistyped, either by hand or in a link.

If you've checked that the link is spelled correctly and the page still isn't working then contact us, and use the form to tell us:

  • the address of the page you were visiting
  • the information you were expecting to see
  • any information contained in the error message you are seeing

If the link you were trying to follow came from GOV.UK then please also tell us

  • the page the broken link is on

How do I report errors I have found on your website?

At the bottom of each page on GOV.UK you can report a problem; if you've found errors (including typos, bugs, or badly formatted pages) then use that link to send us a quick note and we'll investigate that as soon as possible.
