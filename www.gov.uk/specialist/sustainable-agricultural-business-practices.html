Introduction

Successful businesses plan for long-term, sustainable development. Sustainable development means considering the effect of every part of your business on the environment and society. Simply put, your business should have minimal negative effect on the wider world.

While you may have to make some investment to change the way your business operates, the benefits could well outweigh the cost. In any event, you should benefit from lower energy outgoings, greater efficiency, and access to markets keen to support green initiatives.
