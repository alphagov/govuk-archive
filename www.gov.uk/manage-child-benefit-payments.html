Service Manage Child Benefit payments

Not what you're looking for? ↓

You can fill in a form to claim Child Benefit, report a change in circumstances, check when your next Child Benefit payment is due or ask the Child Benefit Office a question.

What you need to know

Start now on the HM Revenue & Customs website.

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know
  • Other ways to apply

You’ll need your:

  • National Insurance number
  • Child Benefit number (if you have one)
  • child’s birth certificate
  • bank or building society account details
  • Takes around 15 minutes
  • National Insurance number required
  • Includes offline steps
