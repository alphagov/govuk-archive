Service Record Driver CPC training you provide

Not what you're looking for? ↓

Record the details of candidates who have completed a Driver Certificate of Professional Competence (CPC) periodic training course. You must be an approved training centre.

What you need to know

Start now on the Department for Transport website

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know

You’ll need your:

  • user ID
  • password

Download ‘A guide to recording periodic training for approved training centres’ (PDF, 1.3MB)

Giving another person access

You can register another person to use the Driver CPC training database if you need them to enter training records. They won’t need to register with the Government Gateway.

Paying to record training

You have to pay to record training details. The fee is £1.25 per driver, per hour of training.

You can pay in 2 ways:

  • by debit or credit card
  • by debit from a pre-funded account

A pre-funded account lets you deposit funds with the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) in advance and pay the fee by debiting the amount directly from your account.

Apply for a pre-funded account

To apply for a pre-funded account, download the terms and conditions, sign the document and send it to DSA.

Download ‘Use of pre-funded accounts for DSA services - terms and conditions’ (PDF, 35KB)
