Blandford HIVE

Serving Blandford and West Moors.

Note: Blandford HIVE is not currently manned. Bovington HIVE is providing cover for this area.

  • tel: 01929 403 185
  • email: bovingtonhive@armymail.mod.uk
  • Information is also available on the Bovington HIVE blog

Blandford Camp lies two miles north-east of the Georgian Town of Blandford Forum in the heart of rural Dorset. The seaside resorts of Bournemouth, Poole and Weymouth are all less than 30 miles away as is the Cathedral City of Salisbury. Within an hour you can reach Southampton (to the east) or the historic city of Bath (to the west). Closer to Blandford are the smaller rural towns of Dorchester, Shaftesbury, Sherborne and Wimborne.

Blandford Forum hosts the annual Great Dorset Steam Fair at the end of August each year. There is a street market every Thursday and Saturday and a Farmers market held once a month. Situated in Blandford are a jobcentre plus (limited opening hours), leisure centre (nearest swimming pool to camp), several dry cleaners, chemists, three supermarkets and gift & souvenir shops. There is also a selection of restaurants and takeaways, pubs, banks and building societies.

The following facilities are available on camp: centrally located HIVE information centre, Army Welfare Service, bowling alley, cafés, cash dispensers, cinema, community centre, families medical centre, serving personnel medical and dental centres, garrison church, grocery store, gurkha temple, gymnasium, george III bar, hairdressers, internet café, nursery, police station, 2 pre-schools, primary school, royal signals museum, Soldiers, Sailors, Airmen and Families Association (SSAFA), thrift shop and two youth centres. The Blandford garrison golfing society is affiliated to Ashley Wood Golf Club two miles from camp and full details may be obtained from the HIVE.

Several children’s groups also run on camp: parent & toddler, rainbows and brownies, youth clubs – age 6 to 8, age 8 to 12 and age 11 upwards, archery and martial arts. Dance classes, football, beavers & scouts, gymnastics, rugby, swimming lessons, and much more are available in the local area.

Bovington HIVE

Serving Bovington Camp and Lulworth.

Bovington Camp in Dorset has been established as a training camp since 1916. The area surrounding Bovington is rural. Amenities within Bovington are limited so travelling to Dorchester (11 miles) or Poole (13 miles) is essential for supermarket shopping and to find all major high street stores. Closer to Bovington is the village of Wool (2 miles). The train station there provides a vital link for travelling to Wareham (7 miles), which has a small high street with various local shops and banks and building societies. The seaside resorts of Bournemouth, Weymouth and Swanage are all less than 25 miles away. Many former military houses in Bovington are now owned by civilians. A community spirit has developed between the military and Civilian population in Bovington and is proving to be successful one with many events being jointly organised and supported by both communities.

The facilities on camp comprise the following: HIVE, AWS Community Office, Sure Start Families Centre, Community Centre with Mother and Toddler room, Londis Shop & Post Office, Cash Machine, Families Medical Centre, Garrison Church, Garrison Welfare Office, Library & PRI shop, Hairdressers, Beehive Café, Launderette, Tank Museum and Gauntlet restaurant, Salvation Army Café, Chinese and Kebab Takeaways, Police Office, Cloudshill Pub/Restaurant, Thrift Shop, Swimming Pool and Gym, Tennis Courts.

Bulford HIVE

Serving Bulford Garrison, Netheravon and Wilton.

Bulford is situated in the beautiful countryside of Wiltshire and forms one part of Tidworth, Netheravon and Bulford Garrison. Bulford lies at the foot of Beacon Hill and the A303 runs along side making it easy to reach such places as Basingstoke, Reading and London as well as Southampton, Bournemouth and Exeter. The village of Amesbury is situated just 3 miles from Bulford Camp and just outside Amesbury are the recognised World Heritage sites of Stonehenge and Woodhenge.

Bulford has a range of facilities which are within walking distance. Bulford shopping precinct has a Spar/NAAFI, post office, electrical store, butchers, military tailors, hairdressers, a café, an Indian and Chinese take away and an Indian restaurant as well as a video rental shop.

The Beeches family centre situated on Bulford Road is a community centre with a range of facilities including a bistro/coffee shop, a conference room, a function room and bar. These are available to hire for christenings etc. There is also a crèche and pre-school, a beautician and unisex hairdressers.

The Haig centre, situated off Bulford Road at the back the Jefferson Estate, is where all the youth activities take place and boasts such facilities as: The Haig Nursery (all day child care), The Getaway Club (after school and holiday care centre for children from Kiwi and Bulford primary schools), The Youth Club (5 to 12 years), Bulford Cubs and Scouts, Parent and Toddler Group, The Thrift Shop and Health House where the midwives and health visitors work from.

Colerne HIVE

Serving Colerne and Corsham.

Colerne is a small very picturesque rural village in the heart of Wiltshire, surrounded by open countryside. The village is within walking distance of the married quarters patch and has a range of small shops including Costcutter, a flower shop, a newsagents and post office as well as Colerne play school and primary school. There is a twice weekly public bus service to Chippenham and a daily bus service to Bath, in addition to which the local unit also provides a free mini bus once a week to Chippenham town centre.

Colerne is home to 21 Signal Regiment (AS) based at Azmighur Barracks, North Colerne. There are a number of facilities on camp, including a gym, a shop (The Market Mace), restaurant (PAYD), thrift shop, unisex hair salon, cash point, community centre, Army Learning Centre and Gooseberry Bush Day Nursery. There are 3 secondary schools within the catchment area, one in Corsham and two in Chippenham. Corsham secondary school has been appointed by the local council as the nearest secondary school, for which the council provides free transport. Public transport is available for the two secondary schools at Chippenham but has to be funded by the individual.

Corsham is approximately 5 miles from Colerne, this idyllic village has a variety of small shops and some excellent restaurants as well as being home to 10 Signal Regiment at the Basil Hill site.

Further afield the town of Chippenham is some 8 miles away and contains a good select ion of high street shops, supermarkets and restaurants. Also nearby is the village of Castle Combe famous for its race track as well as the picturesque village. The historic city of Bath, also approximately 8 miles from Colerne, offers ample shopping, entertainment, and sight seeing opportunities including a large variety of museums and Bath Guild Hall indoor market.

A little further afield is the city of Bristol with its large shopping mall at Cribbs Causeway and the Cabot Circus shopping centre which is more centrally located.

Also within easy reach by car is Swindon with its designer outlet shopping centre.

The M4 is easily accessible from Colerne and there are train stations at both Chippenham and Bath which both offer a direct line service to London Paddington.

Tidworth HIVE

Serving Tidworth Garrison, Perham Down, Shipton Bellinger, Collingbourne Ducis and Andover.

Tidworth is situated in the beautiful countryside of Wiltshire, on the border to Hampshire, and forms one part of the Tidworth, Bulford and Netheravon Garrison. The A303 runs along side Tidworth, making it easy to reach places such as Basingstoke, London, and Reading as well as Southampton, Bournemouth and Exeter. The village of Amesbury is situated 9 miles from Tidworth Camp and just outside Amesbury are the recognised World Heritage sites of Stonehenge and Woodhenge.

Tidworth has a wide range of facilities, most of which are within walking distance. On Station Road, in the Centre of Tidworth, you will find a large Tesco store, hairdressers/ barbers, military supply shops, takeaways, Lloyds Bank, an optician and much more.

Along Pennings Road, the main road going through Tidworth, there is a LIDL, Petrol Station, post office and florist.

The Ashdown Family Centre in Drummer Lane (just off Station Road) is offering a wide range of activities for parents, carers and their children - from “stay and play” sessions to parenting classes, special needs groups and free counselling.

The Tidworth Leisure Centre is situated in Nadder Road, just off Pennings Road, and offers a wide range of activities for all ages. It is equipped with a fitness suite, swimming pool (including flume), tennis courts and squash courts. Also at the Leisure Centre: Café Leisure, the HIVE, Library, Jo Jingles, Caterpillas Day Care (0-5yrs), Community Development Worker, a community hall, Jammie Dodgers After School Club for children from the Clarendon Infant and Junior School, Hairdresser and Purity Beauty salon.

The Windmill Hill Children’s Centre is a Sure Start Children’s Centre, situated on Wylye Road (off Pennings Road), and caters for parents/carers and their children (0-5 yrs). As well as daily activities they offer half term activities and host childminder support groups.

There are several nurseries, crèches and primary schools to choose from, with the nearest secondary schools in Ludgershall (Wellington Academy) or Andover.

Another popular destination, just on Tidworth’s doorstep, is the beautiful New Forest, offering an array of outdoor facilities for daytrips (Beaulieu Motor Museum) or camping with many sites to chose from. For beach lovers, why not take a drive to Bournemouth or even Weymouth? Thanks to Tidworth’s great central location - the possibilities are endless.

Warminster HIVE

Serving Warminster and Devizes.

Warminster is a small market town set between Salisbury and Bath. The main employers are the MOD and Center Parcs on the Longleat Estate. The town has a large park with a boating lake. A children’s play area, a small paddling pool for use in the summer months, a skate board park and tennis courts. Warminster has a sports centre with swimming pool, which also holds activities for children and adults. Longleat Safari Park is only 4 miles from the service family accommodation (SFA).

The Garrison has a pre-school and a creche, both housed in the Garrison Community Centre. The community centre is also a site for various daytime and evening activies. The 10 AEC in Battlesbury Bks, can be used by dependants. Gymnasiums are available for use by dependants (insurance is required) and each of the two camps has a hairdresser, Spar shop and bars.
