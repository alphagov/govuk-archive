Quick answer Contact the Pension Service

Not what you're looking for? ↓

The Pension Service helps with State Pension eligibility, claims and payments.

You can claim online to get your State Pension paid directly into your bank account when you’re within 4 months of your State Pension age.

Claiming your State Pension for the first time
Telephone: 0800 731 7898

Enquiries about State Pension or to report a change of circumstance
Telephone: 08456 060 265
Textphone: 0800 731 7339
Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm

If you’re abroad and have a state pension enquiry contact the International Pension Centre.

You can also make a complaint about the Pension Service.

Write to the Pension Service

If you want to write to the Pension Service, you’ll need to find your local pension centre to get the address. You can also get the phone number. You’ll need to have your postcode.

Pension forecasts

For a pension forecast contact the Future Pension Centre.

You can get independent advice from Citizens Advice.

Local Service Information Points

Find an Information Point to get face-to-face advice or leaflets about:

  • State Pension
  • Pension Credit
  • Attendance Allowance
  • Housing Benefit
  • Council Tax benefit
  • other pensioner entitlements and services

You must make an appointment to get face-to-face advice.
