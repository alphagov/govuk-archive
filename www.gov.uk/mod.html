What we do

Our mission is to protect our country and guarantee its security and independence, as well as helping to project its values and interests abroad. We cannot do everything on our own, so we work closely with our allies and partners. We are working to build more versatile forces for the future, spend our budget wisely and ensure our people have what they need to do what we ask of them.

MOD is a ministerial department , supported by 30 agencies and public bodies.

    The government works to identify the most pressing risks to our security, and put in place the ways and means to address them. Read more

    The government works to promote and protect UK interests internationally, and works with other countries to make progress on issues of mutual concern like trade, climate change, security and economic development. Read more
