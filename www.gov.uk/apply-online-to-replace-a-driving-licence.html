Service Replace a lost, stolen, damaged or destroyed driving licence

Not what you're looking for? ↓

If your driving licence has been lost, stolen, damaged or destroyed you can apply and pay for a new one online. Apply using your Government Gateway ID. If you don’t have one or need to re-register, you’ll get an ID as part of your application.

What you need to know

Start now on the replace your driving licence tool

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know
  • Other ways to apply

You’ll need your driving licence number if you know it and will have to pay £20 online.

If you want to renew the photo on your licence as part of your application you must provide a UK passport issued in the last 5 years.

If you find your old licence after applying for, or receiving, a new licence you must return your old licence to DVLA explaining what’s happened.

Driving licences issued from 19 January 2013 will look different, but your existing licence will still be valid.

  • Proof of identification required
  • 3 years of address details required
  • Debit or credit card required

If you’ve got a photocard driving licence

You can apply by phone if none of your details have changed.

DVLA
Telephone: 0300 790 6801
Monday to Friday, 8am to 7pm
Saturday, 8am to 2pm

You can’t apply by phone if your photo has expired or is about to expire.

To apply by post fill in form D1 ‘application for a driving licence’, available from Post Offices or the DVLA form ordering service.

Send your application and fee to DVLA. Include the part of your licence you still have if only one part is missing.


DVLA
Swansea
SA99 1AB

If your name has changed you’ll also need to send original documents confirming this.

If you’ve got a paper driving licence

If you’ve got a paper driving licence you can apply by post.


DVLA
Swansea
SA99 1AB

You’ll need to:

  • fill in form D1 ‘application for a driving licence’, available from Post Offices or the DVLA form ordering service
  • enclose original documents confirming your identity
  • include a passport-type photo
  • include your paper licence if it’s been damaged
