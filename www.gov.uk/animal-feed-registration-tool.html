Service Animal feed registration

Not what you're looking for? ↓

Animal Feed Registration is part of cross compliance. You must register animal feed with your local council to get your farming subsidy under the Single Payment Scheme. Completing the registration online is quicker, easier and lets you make changes if your circumstances change.
