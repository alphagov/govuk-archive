Inside Government

Websites of all government departments and many other public bodies will be merged into the Inside Government section of www.gov.uk

This will happen in batches, starting soon and finishing in 2014.

On Inside Government you'll find government policy, publications and announcements grouped by topic and organisation.

It will make government information simpler and more open, and save lots of money.

Departments and agencies will be publishing to the site themselves.

All existing web addresses will be redirected, so you don't need to worry about changing your links and bookmarks.

GOV.UK is being built by the Government Digital Service. For more information, see the GDS blog.
