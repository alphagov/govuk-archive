Introduction

HIVES are information centres for forces personnel and their families. This is a list of army HIVES in East Anglia with contact details that includes phone numbers and opening times.

Colchester HIVE

Serving Colchester and Shoeburyness.

The Colchester Families HIVE is situated in the Community Centre Fallowfield Road, located within the Service Families Accommodation (SFA) area. Also in the Community Centre are Army Welfare Service (AWS), Army Families Federation (AFF), youth club, St Nicholas pre-school/nursery and the Musket Club. The youth club is open on Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday and offers various sessions for children aged from 5 years and over. The Musket Club is open lunchtimes, evenings and weekends for service members and their families. Food is available and they run various themed nights throughout the month including bingo.

The Londis shop provides a ‘food to go’ service and a seating area where they provide hot and cold food for most of the day, as well as a cash point, video/DVD hire and baby changing facilities. Next door to the Londis is Garrison Radio where you are welcome to pop in and make a request or even nominate someone for free cake Friday.

Colchester Garrison has recently been redeveloped and now has one central camp called Merville Barracks. Merville Barracks houses the headquarters for 16 Air Assault Brigade, 18 Army Education Centre, the WRVS centre and the living accommodation for unaccompanied or single service personnel.

Situated in the welfare hub of Merville Barracks next to the WRVS centre is a second HIVE office for service personnel. The office is open from 12am to 2pm Monday to Friday. The garrison is situated on the south side of Colchester, 1.5 miles from the town centre. There is a very good bus service to and from town with various stops within the service families estate.

Wattisham HIVE

Serving Wattisham, Hadleigh and Thorington.

Wattisham Flying Station lies in a very beautiful part of rural Suffolk and the attractions of the countryside. It is approximately 5 miles from the town of Needham Market and approximately 8 miles from Stowmarket, both of which can cater for everyday needs. There are swimming pools, leisure centres, cinemas, theatres and many more amenities for both adults and children only a car journey away.

The A14, which is a dual carriageway, will take you to the M11, A1(M), M1 and M6 and all points north. For those wanting to travel to the South the A14 takes you to the A12 and then onto the M25 at Brentwood.

There is a wide variety of amenities at Wattisham Station. They include a gymnasium with multi-gym and cardio-vascular equipment, a swimming pool, squash courts, tennis courts, library, education centre, golf club, gliding club, angling club, clay pigeon shooting club and sub-aqua club.

Hadleigh Outreach Service is open on Wednesdays; call 01473 828995

Wimbish HIVE

Serving Wimbish.

Wimbish is a small rural village in North Essex and is home to 33 Engineer Regiment (EOD) and 101 Engineer Regiment (EOD). The offices of the unit welfare officer, community development worker, defence estates, the Army Learning Centre and the HIVE are all situated around the ‘Village Square’. Here you can also find the Busybees’ childcare centre, the community hall, the Spar and post office where there are coffee and snack facilities.

There are excellent sport facilities provided. These include the gym, which has a basketball, badminton and 2 squash courts. There is also a climbing wall and assault course within camp and everyone has access to the airfield where you can run, walk your dogs, cycle, and also play football and rugby on two well maintained pitches. The community has access to a mobile library and there is a barber shop within the barracks. Both service personnel (all ranks) and their families use the junior ranks club called the ‘Red Wing’. The officers’ and sgt and WOs’ Mess provide the usual facilities. Normal PRI service is available through individual squadrons. Army cadets is available on Carver Barracks. The village hall is a venue for the youth club and various other children’s activities and clubs.

Woodbridge HIVE

Serving Woodbridge, Ipswich, Felixstowe, Sutton Heath Estate for SFAs, Suffolk Coastal District Council.

Woodbridge is an attractive riverside town, steeped in history, on the banks of the river Deben. The town was once a port, noted for its shipbuilding and sail-making industries. Narrow streets hide many historic buildings, including fine examples of Georgian architecture. The town’s centre has some retail and independent shops, dozens of places to eat and drink and some superb antique shops and galleries. Along the quayside is the famous Tide Mill and on the opposite bank of the river, Sutton Hoo, the burial site of Anglo-Saxon kings. This is a great area for bird watching too with lowland heaths, forests, fresh water and salt marshes, reed beds and shingle beaches - many protected for their special value as wildlife habitats - worth visiting at any time of the year.

Woodbridge is not far from the A12, which then leads onto the A14. This is a dual carriageway and will take you to the M11, A1(M), M1 and M6 and all points north. For those wanting to travel South the A14 takes you to the second part of the A12 and then onto the M25 at Brentwood.

Unit facilities

The base has been redeveloped with new junior ranks accommodation, junior ranks mess, WOs’ and Sgts’ accommodation/Mess and officers’ accommodation/Mess facilities.

Also available are a gym/sports hall, outdoor football pitch, rugby pitch, astro turf pitch, medical centre, training wing and is the home of RHQ, 12 HQ Squadron RE, 9 Parachute Sqadron RE, 51 Parachute Squadron RE, 61 Field Squadron (EOD) (part of 33 Engineer Regiment based in Wimbish) and 23 Eng Regt REME Wksp.

The Barnsdale Community Centre houses the following offices:

  • Unit Welfare Officer and SNCO Unit Welfare Officer
  • Woodbridge HIVE Information Centre
  • Station Staff Officer
  • Station Staff Officer’s Chief Clerk
  • Community Development Worker.

The community centre also houses a coffee lounge and various children’s activities.
