Service Check your Driver CPC periodic training hours

Not what you're looking for? ↓

Check how many hours of Driver Certificate of Professional Competence (CPC) periodic training you’ve done, which courses you’ve attended, and create a temporary password for your employer to view your record.

What you need to know

Start now on the Driving Standards Agency website

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know

Training not showing on your record

Contact the training centre if training you’ve done isn’t showing on your record after 5 working days. You should contact the receiver winding up the centre’s finances if you find it’s gone out of business.

Email the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) if the training centre can’t help you.

Driving Standards Agency (DSA)

dcpc.complaints@dsa.gsi.gov.uk

Register as a new user

You need to register to use the service if you’re a new user. You’ll need to give your:

  • driving licence number
  • home postcode

A password for the service will be sent by post to the address on your driving licence.

  • Takes around 5 minutes
