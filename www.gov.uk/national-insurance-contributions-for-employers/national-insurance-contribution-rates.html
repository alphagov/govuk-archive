Part 5: National Insurance Contribution rates

There are 2 types of Class 1 National Insurance Contributions (NICs):

  • primary contributions - what you deduct from your employee’s pay
  • secondary contributions - what you pay towards an employee’s contributions

Both types of contributions depend on:

  • the employee’s National Insurance category
  • the employee’s earnings
