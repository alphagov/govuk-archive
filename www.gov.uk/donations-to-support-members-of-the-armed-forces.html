Quick answer Make a donation to support members of the armed forces

Not what you're looking for? ↓

You can donate to one of the armed forces charities or send someone you know a package.

Send a goodwill parcel

Family and friends of individual service members can send parcels through the Enduring Free Mail Service.

Only use the Enduring Free Mail Service if you know the person you’re sending the parcel to.

Support a service charity

Donate money through one of the following charities:

  • Royal Navy and Royal Marines Charity
  • ABF - The Soldiers’ Charity
  • RAF Benevolent Fund
  • Royal British Legion

Other ways to donate

You can donate money to the Operations Welfare Fund to support staff at the front line. Your donation will be used to buy things that service members have asked for.

Send a cheque made payable to ‘Operations Welfare Fund’ to:

Operations Welfare Fund
J1 Operation HERRICK
PJHQ, Sandy Lane
Northwood
Middlesex
HA6 3HP

You can also donate through uk4u Thanks! - they send boxes with Christmas gifts to all service members stationed overseas.

Armed Forces Day is the last Saturday in June. There are events around the UK where you can show your support.
