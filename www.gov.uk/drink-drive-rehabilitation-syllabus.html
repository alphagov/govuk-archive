Drink-drive rehabilitation syllabus

The drink-drive rehabilitation syllabus sets out what should be covered on drink-drive rehabilitation courses.

Only applies to England, Scotland and Wales

Contents

  1. Introduction
  2. The syllabus
  3. Changes to the syllabus
  4. Further information
  5. See more like this

Introduction

The course can be offered to people convicted of driving or being in charge of a motor vehicle with excess alcohol in their breath, blood or urine or of failing to provide a specimen.

Its objective is to let those people change their behaviour to prevent further offending, which will improve road safety.
