Service Book your practical driving or riding test

Not what you're looking for? ↓

Book your official practical driving or riding test - you’ll need to have passed your theory test. You can book online between 6am and 11:40pm.

What you need to know

Start now on the DSA website

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know
  • Other ways to apply

Practical tests

You can book the following practical tests online:

  • car
  • moped and motorcycle
  • lorry and large vehicle
  • car and large trailer
  • bus and coach
  • taxi
  • specialist vehicles, like tractor, road roller and tracked vehicle
  • approved driving instructor (ADI) driving ability - part 2
  • ADI instructional ability - part 3

Booking

You’ll need your:

  • Great Britain or Northern Ireland driving licence number
  • theory test pass certificate number and date you passed (if needed)
  • credit or debit card - find out how much it costs

You can find your theory test pass certificate number online if you’ve lost your original letter.

If you’re taking a car test and have your driving instructor’s personal reference number you can use this to check they’re available, but can book your test without doing this.

Data protection

You can read the Data Protection Act 1998 fair processing notice for the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) before you start.

  • Takes around 15 minutes
  • Debit or credit card required

Book by phone

You can book your practical driving test by phone by calling DSA.

DSA practical test bookings

Telephone: 0300 200 1122
Welsh language: 0300 200 1133
Textphone: 0300 200 1144
Monday to Friday, 8am to 4pm

Book by post

To book your practical test by post, download the application form and send with the correct fee to DSA.

You can pay by cheque or postal order - make your cheque or postal order payable to the ‘Driving Standards Agency’. You can’t pay by cash.
