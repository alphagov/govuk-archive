Part 4: How to claim

You can apply for Income Support:

  • by post - send a claim form to your local Jobcentre Plus
  • by phone

Download ‘Income Support claim form (A1’) you can complete on screen (PDF, 720KB)
Download ‘Income Support claim form (A1)’ you can print out and complete with a pen (PDF, 720KB)

Jobcentre Plus
Telephone: 0800 055 6688
Textphone: 0800 023 4888
Welsh telephone line: 0800 012 1888
Monday to Friday 8am to 6pm

What you need to know

You can claim Income Support for yourself and your partner, if they haven’t already made a claim.

You need to provide details about your income, where you live and who lives with you.

Appeal a decision

You can appeal the decision about your Income Support if you’re unhappy with it.
