Transport Energy and Environment Statistics notes and guidance

This section provides technical information, notes and guidance and pre-release access relating to Transport Energy and Environment statistics.

Technical information

  • Most of the tables in this series form Chapter 3 of the annual Transport Statistics Great Britain publication.

  • One table ENV0401 is a summary table of greenhouse gas emissions from transport and is updated by the end of March each year after the underlying data have been published by the Department of Energy and Climate Change.

  • Ad-hoc factsheets are published from time to time.

The notes and definitions for all transport energy and environment statistics tables can be found in the document below:

National statistics
