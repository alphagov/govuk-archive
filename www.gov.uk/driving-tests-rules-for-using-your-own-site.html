Driving tests: rules for using your own site

You can host driving tests at your own site if it meets certain rules and is approved by the Driving Standards Agency (DSA).

Only applies to England, Scotland and Wales

Contents

  3. Rules for staff facilities
  4. How to apply
  5. See more like this

Introduction

You can apply to DSA to have your own site approved to be used for the following types of tests:

  • licence acquisition for lorries and car and trailer
  • Driver Certificate of Professional Competence module 4 tests

When a site is approved, DSA sends an examiner to the site to conduct a full programme of tests.

Rules for the site

The site you apply to use must meet the following rules:

  • it must be an off-road manoeuvring area of at least 66 metres (m) by 11 m
  • the manoeuvring area must be line marked following DSA inspection
  • the surface of the manoeuvring area and access to and from will need to be in a reasonable condition
  • the site will need clearly defined access and exit points to allow the safety of other people on site to be managed
  • the manoeuvring area must be free of other vehicles and personnel (other than the examiner and candidate) whilst the test is taking place
  • a suitable barrier is required to replicate a loading bay
  • the sites location must allow for suitable test routes - that is, access to a variety of traffic hazards and road conditions assigned for a test
  • the site must comply with health and safety legislation - visit www.hse.gov.uk for more information
  • the site will need suitable insurance cover
  • the site will need to provide a load securing demonstration trolley if module 4 tests are required for large goods vehicles

Rules for staff facilities

The following must be available for the driving examiner:

  • a private office space with a chair and desk to write up test paperwork
  • a suitable storage facility for the day’s documents and personal belongings
  • male and female staff toilets and kitchen facilities

How to apply

To apply or for more information, contact DSA.

Email: customersites@dsa.gsi.gov.uk
