Part 6: Members of honours committees

Honours committees are made up of senior civil servants (‘official members’) and people who are independent of government (‘independent members’). All honours committees have a majority of members who are independent.

Each honours committee has an independent chairperson.

Honours committees review honours nominations for people involved in specific activities (like arts and media or sport) which are then sent to the main committee.
