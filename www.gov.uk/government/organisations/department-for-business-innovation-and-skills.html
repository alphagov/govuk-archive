What we do

BIS is the department for economic growth. Growth is at the heart of everything we do from investing in skills and education to promoting trade, boosting innovation and helping people start and grow a business.

Department for Business, Innovation & Skills (BIS) is a ministerial department

Read more about BIS

See all of our agencies & partners

We focus on these topics

  1. Business and enterprise

    Growth is key to the UK economy. We want to create the right conditions for companies to thrive and to make it easy for people to start new businesses.

  2. Business law

    We need robust laws on how companies can operate, backed up by transparent auditing, accounting and reporting standards. By modernising and simplifying the law we will foster an open and competitive business environment in the UK.

  3. Economic growth

    Economic growth is our top priority and every part of government is focused on it. We are committed to creating a fairer and more balanced economy. We will create this through private sector growth with jobs evenly balanced across the country.

  4. Trade and investment

    Overseas trade and inward investment are vital for delivering prosperity in the UK. By promoting free trade we will help UK businesses succeed internationally and encourage overseas companies to work with the UK.
