We focus on these topics

  1. Alcohol and drugs

    Alcohol and drug abuse harm lives. When drugs and alcohol are sensibly controlled and the effects of drug-and-alcohol-related crime are minimised, Britain will be a healthier and safer place to live.

  2. Counter terrorism

    The government must be able to respond to the threat posed by terrorism. We aim to prevent attacks by monitoring and disrupting terrorist networks.

  3. Crime and policing

    Crime must be reduced and prevented to ensure people feel safe in their homes and communities. We support visible, responsive and accountable policing by empowering the public and freeing up the police to fight crime.
