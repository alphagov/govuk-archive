The MOD military estate is 1% of the UK's landmass. We manage all grounds on behalf of the MOD, providing accommodation for service personnel and their families, as well as organising training and volunteer areas.

Responsibilities

We are responsible for:

  • providing cost-effective facilities for the armed forces and their families
  • managing the acquisition, development and sale of land and buildings
  • supporting related services like maintenance, catering and health and safety for the estate

Priorities

In 2012 to 2013 our priorities are:

  • reducing our running costs
  • increasing the sale of surplus land and buildings
  • achieving full Defence Infrastructure Organisation (DIO) operating capability by 2013

Who we are

We are managed from our HQ in the West Midlands by the Chief Executive DIO Andrew Manley supported by Chief Operating Officer David Olney. The DIO’s most important partnership is with industry.
