Statistical data set New households and recent movers

Organisations:
  • Department for
    Communities and
    Local Government
Published:
14 November 2012
Series:
English housing survey

Data on the mobility, demographics and tenancies of new households and ones that have recently moved.

Web tables on: mobility among all households; length of residence; demographic characteristics of movers; movement between tenures; movement into and out of tenures; tenancy deposits.
