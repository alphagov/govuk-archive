Biography

The Rt Hon Desmond Swayne is a Government Whip and Lord Commissioner of HM Treasury who is the Member of Parliament for the constituency of New Forest West, in Hampshire.

Education

Desmond was educated at Drumley House Preparatory School at Mossblown in South Ayrshire and Bedford School. He studied Theology at St Marys College at the University of St Andrews.

Political career

Following the election of David Cameron as leader of the Conservative Party in December 2005, Desmond was appointed as his Parliamentary Private Secretary. He maintained his role as a PPS in government until appointed a Government Whip in September 2012.

On 11 June 2011, it was announced Desmond would be appointed a Privy Counsellor in the Queen’s 2011 Birthday Honours List On 13 July 2011, he was duly sworn of Council.

Career outside politics

From 1980 to 1981 Desmond was a school teacher in Economics at Charterhouse, then Wrekin College from 1982 to 1987. From 1988 to 1996, he was Manager of Risk Management Systems at the Royal Bank of Scotland.
