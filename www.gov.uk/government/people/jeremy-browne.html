Biography

Jeremy Browne was appointed Minister of State for Crime Prevention in September 2012. He is the Liberal Democrat MP for Taunton Deane.

Education

Jeremy was educated at Bedales School and Nottingham University, where he studied politics and was President of the Students’ Union.

Political career

  • Shadow Minister for the Foreign & Commonwealth Office (FCO) 2005 to 2007
  • Shadow Minister for Home Affairs 2007
  • Shadow Chief Secretary to the Treasury 2007 to 2010
  • Minister of State at the FCO May 2010 to September 2012
  • Minister of State at the Home Office September 2012 to present

Career outside politics

Jeremy worked for financial consultancy Dewe Rogerson, for Edelman Communications Worldwide and for ReputationInc, specialising in corporate reputation management.

  • Home Office

Minister of State for Crime Prevention

The minster is responsible for:

  • the National Crime Agency and the Crime and Courts Bill
  • drugs
  • organised crime and the Serious Organised Crime Agency
  • financial crimes (fraud, asset recovery)
  • forensic science
  • volume, acquisitive and business crime (including burglary, motor vehicle crime, retail crime, cash vehicles in transit, wildlife/rural crime, metal theft)
  • crime strategy
  • designing-out crime
  • antisocial behaviour
  • public health
  • drugs strategy
  • alcohol (including Licensing Act and powers of police and local authorities) and alcohol strategy
  • violent crime, violent offender orders, Victims Fund
  • homelessness
  • community activism
  • youth crime and youth violence
  • domestic violence (including forced marriage, honour killing and female genital mutilation; sexual violence; sex offenders; child sex offenders review; prostitution; lapdancing; violence against women and girls; sexualisation of young people; women’s safety and fear of crime)
  • missing persons
  • hate crime
  • online child protection (including Child Exploitation and Online Protection Centre; UK Council for Child Internet Safety; obscenity and internet pornography)
