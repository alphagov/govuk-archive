News New Year Honours for Service personnel and Defence civilians

Organisations:
  • Ministry
    of Defence
Published:
29 December 2012

120 UK Service personnel have been granted state honours by Her Majesty The Queen in the annual New Year Honours list.

42 civilians have also been honoured either for work in the Ministry of Defence or in other aspects of UK Defence.

The 162 Defence personnel named in the New Year Honours list for 2013 are as follows:
