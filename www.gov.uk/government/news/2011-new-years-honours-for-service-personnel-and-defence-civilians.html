News 2011: New Year's Honours for Service personnel and Defence civilians

Organisations:
  • Ministry
    of Defence
Published:
31 December 2010

121 UK Service personnel have been granted state honours by Her Majesty The Queen in her annual New Year's Honours list.

46 civilians have also been honoured either for work in the Ministry of Defence or in other aspects of UK Defence.

The full Defence New Year’s Honours list for 2011 is below:
