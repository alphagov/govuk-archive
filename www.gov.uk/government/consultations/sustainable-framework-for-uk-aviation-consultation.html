Detail

The scoping document frames the debate and asks a series of questions to initiate a dialogue with a wide range of stakeholders on the future direction of aviation policy.

The government’s response to the Committee on Climate Change and accompanying documents provide important additional evidence for stakeholders to consider when formulating their responses to the scoping document. Due to the delay to publication of this material, we accepted responses to the scoping document until 20 October 2011.
