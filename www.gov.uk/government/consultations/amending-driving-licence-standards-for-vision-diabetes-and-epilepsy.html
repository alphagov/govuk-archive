Closed consultation Amending driving licence standards for vision, diabetes and epilepsy

Organisations:

    for Transport

Policies:

This consultation ran between 3 February 2011 and 28 April 2011

We are analysing your responses

Visit this page again soon to download the outcome to this public feedback.

Detail

This consultation is being issued for comments on changes to the minimum standards for vision, diabetes and epilepsy in relation to driving. Driver licensing in Great Britain is carried out by Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency (DVLA) and in Northern Ireland by the Driver and Vehicle Agency (DVA). This document relates to licensing standards across the United Kingdom; any reference to DVLA applies to DVA.

This document seeks views on changes to the medical standards for vision, diabetes and epilepsy.
