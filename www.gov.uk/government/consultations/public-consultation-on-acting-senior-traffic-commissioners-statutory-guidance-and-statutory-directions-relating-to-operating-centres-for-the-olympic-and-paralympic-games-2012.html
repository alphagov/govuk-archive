Closed consultation Public consultation on acting Senior Traffic Commissioner's statutory guidance and statutory directions relating to operating centres for the Olympic and Paralympic Games 2012

Organisations:
  • Department
    for Transport
Published:
29 December 2011
Policies:
Managing, improving and investing in the road network and Providing effective regulation of freight transport

This consultation ran between 29 December 2011 and 3 February 2012

We are analysing your responses

Visit this page again soon to download the outcome to this public feedback.

Detail

The Local Transport Act 2008 requires the Senior Traffic Commissioner to give guidance and directions as to the way in which the traffic commissioners will approach the exercise of their statutory functions. This consultation is taking place to allow the traffic commissioners support staff, stakeholders and the wider industry the opportunity to comment on the implementation of legislation and procedures that are proposed to be adopted by the traffic commissioners in relation to applications by goods vehicle operators leading up to and during the Olympic and Paralympic Games 2012.
