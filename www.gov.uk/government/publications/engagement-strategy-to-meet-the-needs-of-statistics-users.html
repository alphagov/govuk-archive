Detail

This document sets out the overall strategy that analysts in the Department for Communities and Local Government have adopted for meeting the needs of all those persons and bodies who use the Department’s official statistics.

It describes the vision behind our strategy, its objectives and the principles upon which it is based.

It also identifies our various users, lists the various ways in which we will engage with them and describes the various ways in which our statistics are used.
