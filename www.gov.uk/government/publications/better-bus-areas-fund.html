Summary

Winners of the Better Bus Area Fund 2012 have now been announced and details of the successful bids can be downloaded from this page.

In total, the grants come to £70 million, an increase of £20 million on the figure announced on 7 December 2011. This is in recognition of the quality of the bids received.

The aim of the fund is to increase bus patronage in busy urban areas, and the department’s aims of creating growth and cutting carbon. Funding will be provided for 24 of the 50 applicants.
