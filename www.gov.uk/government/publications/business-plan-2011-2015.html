Detail

The MODs 2012-15 Business Plan sets out the programme of work we plan to carry out over the next three years to support the Coalition Governments objectives. It also contains information on our structure and budget, and the way we measure our performance. The full plan is available to download using the link below.

The Structural Reform Plan section can be viewed on the updated Number 10 Transparency website (please see External links), with information on the current status of each action, and explanations for any deadlines missed, updated monthly.

This years Business Plan is an update of the Business Plan published in May 2011, which can be accessed below . In addition, the annexes detail additional actions that compliment the SRP and support cross government priorities, and list completed actions from the 2011 Business Plan.

The changes from the 2011 Business Plan are highlighted in the attached 2011 Business Plan revisions document. The previous version of the Number 10 Transparency website, which will shortly be updated with a final set of updates for May 2012, can be accessed from External links.

Monthly updates on the implementation of the Structural Reform Plan element of the MOD Business Plan are also available below.
