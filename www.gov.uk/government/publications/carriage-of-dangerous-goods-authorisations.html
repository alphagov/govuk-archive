Detail

Under Regulation 9(2) (rail) and 12(1) (roads) of The Carriage of Dangerous Goods and Use of Transportable Pressure Equipment Regulations 2009 (as amended), the Secretary of State for Transport, may issue written authorisations that allow for deviations from the requirements of European Agreement concerning the International Carriage of Dangerous Goods by Road (ADR) and Regulations concerning the International Carriage of Dangerous Goods by Rail (RID).

These authorisations carry conditions and time limits, and apply to carriage within GB only; they can be withdrawn by the competent authority at any time by means of a written notice.
