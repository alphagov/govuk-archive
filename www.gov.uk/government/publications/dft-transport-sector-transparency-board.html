Aim of the board

To be the focal point for the transparency agenda in the transport sector and to act as an effective advisory and challenge body to ensure that transparency principles are incorporated into the business as usual processes of the department. To achieve the benefits of transparency for end-users of public services, for public sector professionals and for businesses. To assist the department and its delivery partners to achieve their wider objectives. To enable growth creation through the release of open transport data and to facilitate exciting new user-focused services and applications.
