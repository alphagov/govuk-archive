Communication with in-flight supplies inspectors

Please send all non-urgent communication to inspectors on their email addresses listed below. Please provide as much detail as possible in your message and inspectors will endeavour to provide a prompt response. If regulated suppliers do not have a named email contact, a message to the office email address transec.cargo@dft.gsi.gov.uk will allow for the message to be passed on to the appropriate inspector. Inspectors would be grateful for the assistance of the industry in this matter.
