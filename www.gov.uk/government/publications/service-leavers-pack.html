Detail

Welcome to the Service Leaver’s Pack webpage. Under current arrangements, all Service Leavers will receive a Service Leaver’s Pack from the Termination Cell, SPVA(G), about 9 months prior to their termination date. This pack is intended to offer guidance and advice prior to and after leaving Regular Service. If you are eligible for a pack and have not yet received one, you can submit a request through the JPAC Enquiry Centre on 0141 224 3600, Freephone 0800 0853600 or MOD 94560 3600.

The pack contains information about your pension (if eligible), Resettlement, Reserve Liability and Support Agencies. Personnel leaving on Redundancy should also read the document ‘Addendum to the Service Leavers’ Guide for those leaving the Service under Redundancy terms’ via the Document Link below. Any queries regarding the content of a document should be directed to the document sponsor. SPVA is not responsible for the contents of non-SPVA sponsored documents.

Of particular importance to Service Leavers is the SPVA Pension Form 1. This form should be completed at the 6 month point prior to your last day of Service using the Pen Form 1 link on your JPA account. If you do not have access to JPA you must compete the manual form, no later than 6 months before your last day of service, and it must be returned to SPVA (address given on the form). The completion of this form is a legal requirement and failure to do so will result in a delay of the receipt of Terminal Benefits and pension if eligible..
