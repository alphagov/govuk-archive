About the organisation structure charts and data

Organisation structure charts (organograms) include:

  • structure of the department/body down to directorate level
  • positions of all Senior Civil Servant (SCS) Pay Band 2 and above (or equivalent)
  • names of SCS Pay Band 2 and above
  • salaries of all Pay Band 2 and above
  • job titles of SCS Pay Band 1 and 1A (or equivalent)
  • reporting numbers of staff within directorates (FTE) and total salary costs of SCS1 teams

Organisational data CSV files include:

  • SCS staff posts - name, grade, job title, team function, parent department, organisation, unit, contact details, reports, salary cost of reports

Salary disclosure data CSV files include:

  • SCS staff salaries - name, grade, job title, FTE, parent department, organisation, unit, actual pay floor, actual pay ceiling
  • Junior staff FTE - parent department, organisation, unit, reporting SCS, grade, number of posts FTE
