Detail

Introduction

An independent report of a study chaired by Sir Roy McNulty and commissioned by the Secretary of State for Transport. The report was jointly sponsored by DfT and the Office of Rail Regulation to examine the opportunities and barriers to improve the value for money of GB rail for taxpayers, passengers and freight customers.
