Detail

Volume 3 contains advice for staff and contractors on health and safety risks associated with high risk infrastructure on the defence estate.

JSP 375 Volume 3 is produced by Defence Infrastructure Organisation (DIO) formerly Defence Estates, under a remit by Safety, Sustainable Development and Continuity Division.

NOTE: Chapters 1 and 2 have been updated as of June 2011. These two Chapters have been combined into a single document and can be accessed by either the Chapter 1 or Chapter 2 links.

Note: DIO Policy Instructions have now been included; these provide further background information on changes to Chapters and Safety Rule Books.
