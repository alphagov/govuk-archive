Detail

The purpose of this document is to explain the contents and operation of the Environmental Management element of MODs Acquisition Safety and Environmental Management System (ASEMS). This element is known as the Project-Oriented Environmental Management System (POEMS). The other element of ASEMS is the Project-Oriented Safety Management System (POSMS) which has a separate, although closely related, manual.

The POEMS Manual includes all Core Procedures (EMP01 - EMP08), Support Procedures (SSP01 - SSP03) and Assurance and Audit Procedures (AAP01 - AAP04).

POEMS is now at Version 2.3 and the documents below will be updated shortly. For access to POEMS V2.3 please access via the POEMS Process Maps on the right.
