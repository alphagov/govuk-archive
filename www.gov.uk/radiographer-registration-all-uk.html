Licence Radiographer registration (All UK)

It is not currently possible to apply online for this licence. Please contact your local authority.

Overview

To practise as a radiographer, you need to register with the Health and Care Professions Council (HCPC).

This includes using the protected titles:

  • radiographer
  • diagnostic radiographer
  • therapeutic radiographer
