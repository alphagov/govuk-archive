Service Pass Plus approved driving instructor (ADI) services

Not what you're looking for? ↓

Register as a Pass Plus approved driving instructor (ADI), order your refill packs and track whether your pupil’s certificate has been posted.

What you need to know

Start now on the Department for Transport website

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know
  • Other ways to apply

You’ll also need your:

  • online user ID from the Driving Standards Agency (DSA)
  • password

It costs:

  • £37 to register
  • £29 for a refill pack

The online service is available from 6am to 11:30pm.

Your user ID and password

Your user ID can be found on all letters sent to you by DSA. It’s not the same as your ADI personal reference number.

You can email DSA if you’ve never had a user ID or have lost yours. You’ll need to include 2 of these in your email:

  • your ADI number
  • your driving licence number
  • your date of birth

DSA helpline

irdt@dsa.gsi.gov.uk

Rules for passwords

You have to change your password every 60 days. You can’t use any of your 5 previously used passwords.

Your password must be at least 9 characters long. It must include at least 3 of these 4 types of characters:

  • uppercase characters, eg A B C
  • lowercase characters, eg a b c
  • numeric digits, eg 1 2 3
  • special characters, eg £ % &
  • Takes around 15 minutes
  • Debit or credit card required

Register or order by phone

To register or order refill packs by phone, call the DSA Pass Plus helpline.

DSA Pass Plus helpline
0115 936 6504
Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm

Register or order by post

To register or order refill packs by post, send a cheque or postal order made payable to ‘Driving Standards Agency’ to DSA.

You’ll need to include your ADI personal reference number and address.


DSA
PO Box 280
Newcastle upon Tyne
NE99 1FP
