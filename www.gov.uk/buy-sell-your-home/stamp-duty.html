Part 7: Stamp Duty

If you buy a property for more than £125,000, you pay Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT) of between 1% and 7% on the whole purchase price.

If you pay £125,000 or less, you don’t pay any SDLT.

See Stamp Duty rates.
